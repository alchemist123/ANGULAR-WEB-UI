Reports
You can view students answers for your questions/activities here.



Pre Assessment

Report shows the level of knowledge of the student about the topics and concepts included in the lesson; before the lesson is being taught. Also looks into the level of knowledge about previous lessons including that of earlier classes.

Self Assessment

Report shows the level of understanding of the topic or concept taught in the lesson. Whether the student is able to answer by himself or takes the help of the teacher is understood here.

Activity Assessment

Report shows the engagement of students in an activity. Also understands if he is a peer able to assess others or not.

Post Assessment

Report shows the level of knowledge acquired after completing the lesson including activities. This is the final report on the lesson.
