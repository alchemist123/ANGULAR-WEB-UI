Preparing students for the Second Machine Age

“Technology is a gift of God. After the gift of life, it is perhaps the greatest of God’s gifts.
It is the mother of civilizations, of arts and sciences.”- Freeman Dyson






Technology has started transforming the economy much more than it did a century ago when electricity transformed manufacturing or computers and the internet. We are passing through manage of rapidly evolving technologies. Now students need to reshape the way they learn their lessons by integrating the core subjects Science, Arts and Math with Technology and Engineering. Students have to play around with machines and be experts in training them to communicate. They have to be data-intensive and technology-intensive. Students should be emphatic and understand the problems faced by human beings. Investigate and realize the causes of the problems. Learn about existing solutions and the reasons why they still exist. They should become adept in making decisions by looking at the data. Build creative solutions while in schools and colleges. Get recognized and build their own wellbeing.



The STE(A)M Approach

StemClass approaches learning through a pedagogy that integrates STEAM subjects in an interesting way.

Science

Learn Physics, Chemistry and Biology like a scientist, who is passionate in research and innovation. Involve in different types of activities including experiential and multidisciplinary.

Technology

Focus on connecting the learner with IR 4.0 technologies, understanding the history and becoming curious in exploring more.

Engineering

Curiosity leads them to develop solutions by using the latest engineering techniques.

Arts

Create and design your imaginations using the latest tools.

Mathematics

Learn not only about shapes and structures but also to analyse and interpret with data.

Features

Competency-Based Assessments

Practice CBS through each lesson so that decisions can be taken based on reports.

Digital Skills

Acquire Digital Skills at your leisure time and build digital products that can be useful for others around you. Become a StudentPreneur at a very young age.

Personalised Learning

Customized learning approach that focuses on each student’s strengths, skills, needs and interests. Students get a learning plan based on what they know and how they learn best.

Studentpreneur

Studentpreneur practices two roles at the same time. First, you are a student in school, learning the academic lessons. Second, you acquire digital skills you are passionate about after school hours and create a valuable product and promote the product online. Become an entrepreneur at an early age. It is easier to start off at an early age when you are a student rather than when you are an employee.
